  Attributes ×10                                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                    
  One score (1–6) each for: Strength, Endurance, Agility, Dexterity, Attunement, Sight, Intelligence, Perception, Will, Presence                                                                    

  ---
  Characteristics ×11

  Each has a rank (bought with XP) and a rating (rank + two contributing attributes):
  Martial, Marksmanship, Initiative, Speed, Stamina, Potential, Arcane, Psychic, Command, Dodge, Grit

  ---
  Skills ×17

  Each has a rank and rating (rank + two attributes). Split into Untrained (everyone starts at rank 1) and Trained (rank 0 without training):
  Acrobatics, Athletics, Awareness, Deceive, Eerie, Impose, Influence, Investigate, Subterfuge, Occultism (untrained) — Access, Alchemy, Electronics, Magitek, Piloting, Ritual, Shop (trained)

  ---
  Combat Values (all derived)

  - Initiative (rolled each encounter: dice pool = Initiative rating)
  - Evasion (base + armor bonuses)
  - Armor Rating / Magic Armor (from equipped armor)
  - Speed / movement range
  - AP per turn (base 4)
  - Stamina capacity (equipment bulk limit)

  ---
  Advances

  A list of purchased advances (name, tier, XP cost, HP/FP granted) — this is the primary progression record.

  ---
  Powers

  - Spells: Known list + currently equipped slots (limit from Arcane advances)
  - Prayers: Access comes from equipped reliquary (domain-dependent), plus tap effects
  - Psychic / Martial / Command powers: Learned permanently; track each by name and tier

  ---
  Equipment

  - Weapons (damage, pen, bulk, hands, range, fire mode)
  - Armor (armor rating, magic armor, evasion, bulk)
  - Catalyst (weight category, charge, spell damage die)
  - Reliquary (will, favor, domain)
  - Carried items
  - Current bulk total vs. Stamina capacity
  - Currency (Ƒ)

  ---
  Status Conditions (tick-box area)

  Blinded, Dazed, Disoriented, Exposed, Panicked, Shred (×), Slowed (×), Staggered, Still, Stunned, Vulnerable, Disrupt (×)

  ---
  The main design tension is between characteristics and skills — both have a rank and a derived rating, so each needs two boxes. The advance list is essentially a character's build history and
  doubles as the HP/FP ledger, so it warrants a dedicated table rather than just a running total.
