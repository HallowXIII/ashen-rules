# Character Sheet Summary

Updated 2026-04-19 to reflect current rules state.

---
## Identity & Origin

- Name, race, subtype (e.g. Echo / Shade), background

---
## Attributes ×10

One score (1–6) each for: **Strength**, **Endurance**, **Dexterity**, **Agility**, **Attunement**, **Sight**, **Intelligence**, **Perception**, **Will**, **Presence**

---
## Characteristics ×11

Each has a rank (1–6, bought with XP) and a rating (rank + two contributing attributes):

| Characteristic | Attributes | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Martial | STR + AGI | Melee/thrown attack rolls |
| Marksmanship | DEX + PER | Ranged attack rolls |
| Initiative | SGT + INT | Turn order |
| Speed | AGI + END | Movement range = 2 + Speed rating |
| Stamina | STR + END | Bulk capacity |
| Potential | ATT + WIL | Charge capacity; resist harmful magic |
| Arcane | ATT + INT | Arcane casting rolls |
| Psychic | SGT + WIL | Psychic casting rolls |
| Command | PRS + WIL | Divine casting rolls; charm/command |
| Dodge | AGI + PER | Evasion = ½ Dodge rating (rounded down) |
| Grit | END + WIL | Resist pain, mental effects |

---
## Skills ×17

Each has a rank and rating (rank + two attributes).

**Untrained** (everyone starts at rank 1): Acrobatics, Athletics, Awareness, Deceive, Eerie, Impose, Influence, Investigate, Subterfuge, Occultism

**Trained** (rank 0 without training): Access, Alchemy, Electronics, Magitek, Piloting, Ritual, Shop

---
## Vital Resources

| Stat | Base | Growth | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| HP | 10 | From advances | Damage capacity |
| FP (Focus Points) | 10 | From advances | Fuel for powers |
| EP (Essence Points) | 10 | +1 per 1000 XP | Cheat death (5 EP), rest recovery (1 EP), end effects (1 EP). Recover 3 on long rest. |
| XP | 0 | Earned in play | Spent on advances |
| Currency (ƒ) | Varies | Earned/looted | Feathers — maintenance costs, purchases |

---
## Combat Values (all derived)

| Stat | Formula | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Initiative | Roll Initiative rating each encounter | Determines turn order |
| Evasion | ½ Dodge rating + armor Eva bonuses | Threshold to be hit |
| Armor | From equipped armor | Reduces physical damage |
| Ward | From equipped armor + other sources | Reduces magical damage |
| Movement Speed | 2 + Speed rating | Squares per move action |
| AP per turn | 4 (base) | Spent on actions |
| Bulk capacity | Stamina rating | Max Bulk of equipped/wielded items |
| Charge capacity | Potential rating | Max Charge of equipped/wielded items |
| Carrying capacity | Stamina × 2 (×3 for Ironhands/Warboars) | Max Bulk of all carried items |

---
## Advances

A list of purchased advances (name, tier, XP cost, HP/FP granted). This is the primary progression record.

---
## Powers

- **Spells (Arcane):** Known list + currently memorized/equipped slots (limit from Arcane advances)
- **Prayers (Divine):** Access from equipped reliquary (aspect-dependent) + tap effects. Track favor per reliquary.
- **Psychic powers:** Learned permanently; track by name and tier. May be augmented.
- **Martial exploits:** Learned permanently; track by name and tier.
- **Command powers:** Learned permanently; track by name and tier.
- **Marksmanship exploits:** Learned permanently; track by name and tier.

---
## Equipment

For each item, track:

**Weapons:**
- Name, type, tier
- Damage, Pen, Bulk, Hands, Inc
- Range, Fire Mode (ranged only)
- Technology, Treatment, Enchantments (if any)
- Keywords
- Base price (for maintenance calculation)

**Armor** (up to 3 slots: C, P, O; or C+P + O):
- Name, tier, profile (C/P/O/C+P)
- Armor, Ward, Evasion, Bulk, Charge
- Material variant + resistance (if any)
- Enchantments (if any)
- Base price

**Catalyst:**
- Name, tier, type (Arcane/Psychic), class (Light/Medium/Heavy)
- Damage, Inc, Casting Bonus, Bulk, Charge, Hands
- Specialized school (if any)
- Enchantments (if any)

**Reliquary:**
- Name, tier, class
- Damage, Inc, Will, Aspects
- Current Favor

**Other:**
- Shields, consumables, general gear
- Current currency (ƒ)

**Totals:**
- Total Bulk (equipped + wielded) vs Bulk capacity
- Total Charge (equipped + wielded) vs Charge capacity
- Total carried Bulk vs carrying capacity
- Total equipment value (for maintenance cost = 10% per intermission)

---
## Status Conditions (tick-box area)

**Standard:** Blinded, Dazed, Disoriented, Exposed, Panicked, Staggered, Stunned, Vulnerable

**Stacking:** Disrupt (×), Shred (×), Slowed (×)

**Movement:** Prone, Immobilized, Restrained

**Persistent:** Frightened (of X), Weakened, Dominated, Exhausted, Poisoned, Diseased, Bleeding, Burning

**Equipment-related:** Frozen (from Freezing keyword — stunned, save ends)

---
## Notes / Design Considerations

- Characteristics and skills both have rank + rating; each needs two boxes on the sheet.
- The advance list doubles as the HP/FP ledger — warrants a dedicated table.
- Equipment section needs enough space for modifications (material, tech, treatment, enchantments) per item.
- Favor tracking per reliquary (not per character) — needs a small sub-box per reliquary.
- Maintenance cost total is useful as a running tally.
- Condition area should have tick-boxes for standard conditions plus write-in space for stacking values.
