# Ashen RPG — Ritual & Recipe Design Guidelines

Reference document for designing rituals and alchemical recipes.

---

## Tier Scaling

| Tier | Difficulty | Magnitude | Component Cost (Ƒ) | Casting Time | Effect Scope |
|------|-----------|-----------|---------------------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | 3-4 | 2-3 | 50-200 | 5-10 min | Single target, local effect |
| 2 | 5-6 | 4-5 | 200-500 | 10-30 min | Small area, moderate duration |
| 3 | 7-8 | 6-7 | 500-1500 | 30 min - 1 hr | Large area, lasting effect |
| 4 | 9-10 | 8-9 | 1500-4000 | 1-4 hours | Powerful transformation, permanent minor effects |
| 5 | 11-12 | 10-12 | 4000-10000 | 4-12 hours | Reality-altering, permanent major effects |
| 6 | 13+ | 13+ | Priceless / unique | 1+ days | Campaign-defining, legendary |

**Difficulty reduction:** Each point of available power beyond the ritual's magnitude reduces difficulty by 1, to a floor equal to the ritual's tier.

---

## Sacrifice Types

- **Components** (T1-T4): Money and materials. Standard cost. No lasting impact beyond the price. Consumed whether or not the ritual succeeds.
- **Essence** (T3-T5): Caster takes strain or a long-lasting debuff. Used for rituals that channel dangerous forces. The debuff persists for days or weeks. Represents physical/spiritual cost to the caster.
- **Influence** (T4-T6): Requires a concentration of specific aspects in the environment or sacrificed objects. For example, a ritual requiring Mist influence of magnitude 5 must be cast in a strongly magical environment (leyline, ancient temple) or the caster must sacrifice a magical object that provides that level of aspect resonance. Influence is consumed — the environmental attunement fades or the object is destroyed.

---

## Aspect Requirements

- **T1-T3**: No aspect requirements. Accessible to anyone with the skill and materials.
- **T4**: May require one aspect as Influence. Without the required aspect, difficulty increases by +2.
- **T5-T6**: Require 1-2 specific aspects as Influence. Must be provided by environment or sacrificed objects.

**Aspect list:** Ash (destruction, desolation), Crown (law, domination), Forge (crafting, heat, origin), Key (opening, summoning), Mist (magic, the sublime essence), Scarab (rebirth, cycles), Skull (death, the end of things), Moon (mind, matter), Maiden (nature)

---

## Skills

- **Ritual** — Arcane and structured Psychic magic. Systematic, reproducible. Failed checks = wasted materials, but predictable consequences.
- **Occultism** — Divine and esoteric Psychic magic (Fate, Void domains). Communion with entities. Failed checks may have unpredictable or dangerous consequences.
- **Alchemy** — Exclusive skill for all recipes. Ritual and Occultism do not apply. Covers potions, poisons, reagents, explosives.

## Design Checklist

1. [ ] Difficulty and Magnitude within tier band?
2. [ ] Sacrifice type appropriate to power level?
3. [ ] Casting time proportional to effect?
4. [ ] Clear effect description with measurable outcomes?
5. [ ] Failure consequence defined (or "standard" = consumables lost)?
6. [ ] Skill specified (Ritual, Occultism, or Alchemy)?
7. [ ] Aspects required only if T4+?
8. [ ] Difficulty modifiers listed for variable-scope rituals?
9. [ ] Does the ritual fill a narrative or mechanical niche not covered by combat powers?

## Universal Reagent Reference

- Readily obtainable universal reagents cap at 6 magnitude (T3)
- The Philosopher's Reagent recipe (T5, Alchemy) produces 8 magnitude reagents
- Higher-magnitude reagents are unique/legendary and must be found or quested for
